about

Fortis Hospital is a well-known healthcare institution that offers a wide range of medical services. It is part of the Fortis Healthcare network, which is one of the largest and most respected healthcare providers in India.

Fortis Hospitals are equipped with advanced medical technology and staffed by experienced healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, and support staff.

The hospital offers services in various specialties such as cardiology, orthopedics, neurology, oncology, and more. It is known for its commitment to providing high-quality patient care, innovative treatments, and a patient-centric approach.

why fortis?

At Fortis, we are committed to providing world-class healthcare services with the highest standards of excellence and care. Here’s why you should choose Fortis for your healthcare needs:

jci & nabh accredited

Our hospital is proud to be accredited by both the Joint Commission International (JCI) and the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH).

291 hospital beds

With 291 hospital beds, we offer comprehensive medical services to a large number of patients, ensuring that everyone receives the attention and care they deserve.

11 OTs & 108 ICU Beds

Our state-of-the-art facilities include 11 advanced operating theatres and 108 intensive care unit (ICU) beds. This allows us to perform a wide range of surgeries and provide critical care for patients with severe or life-threatening conditions.

robotic surgeries

Fortis is at the forefront of medical technology with our robotic surgery capabilities. These minimally invasive procedures result in less pain, reduced recovery times, and better outcomes for our patients.

frequently asked question

Why should I consider cancer treatment at Fortis Hospital?

Consider Fortis Hospital for cancer treatment due to its experienced oncologists, state-of-the-art facilities, multidisciplinary approach, supportive care services, and positive patient reviews. The hospital’s accreditations and quality standards, along with its accessible location, make it a compelling option for comprehensive and quality cancer care.

How much does cancer treatment cost in India compared to other countries?

Cancer treatment costs in India are significantly lower than in many Western countries. The cost advantage can be up to 50-70% less for similar procedures and treatments. India’s healthcare system offers cost-effective treatments, including surgery, chemotherapy, and radiation therapy, attracting medical tourists from around the world

How can I seek an appointment with an oncologist at Fortis Hospital?

To seek an appointment with a Fortis Hospital oncologist, fill out their online enquiry form, or email your request to the provided address. Don’t forget to share relevant medical records for better evaluation. The hospital will respond with available dates for your oncology consultation.

Are the cancer treatment facilities and technology up to international standards at Fortis Hospital?

Fortis Hospital Mulund is known for maintaining cancer treatment facilities and technology up to international standards. It offers state-of-the-art medical equipment, advanced therapies, and experienced oncologists, providing patients with quality care that exceeds international norms in cancer treatment.
